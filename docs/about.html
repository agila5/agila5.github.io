About me

  • Current Position: I am an Assistant Professor (Ricercatore SECS-S/01, Legge 240/10 tipo A) at the Department of Mathematics of Politecnico di Milano. I am a member of the MOX laboratory of mathematical modelling and scientific computing. Currently, I’m working on a project named GRINS: Growing Resilient, INclusive and Sustainable which is part of the Next Generation EU program (National Recovery and Resilience Plan).
  • Research Interests: Spatial and spatio-temporal models for data on linear networks.
  • Email Address: andrea.gilardi@polimi.it
  • Present Address: Politecnico di Milano, Department of Mathematics, Piazza Leonardo da Vinci, 32 – 20133 Milano - building 14 “Nave” Campus Bonardi, Italy.
